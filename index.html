About Us

Ajay Machine Tools was established in 1997 in Rohtak, Haryana. We specialize in manufacturing powder coating machinery and industrial engineering equipment.

With decades of experience in machine manufacturing, Ajay Machine Tools focuses on delivering efficient and durable machines tailored to the needs of industrial clients. We provide complete end-to-end support, from the initial machine design to full installation and setup at your facility.
